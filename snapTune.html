Overview

I collaborated with four others to create a revolutionary app that helps people discover new music. As music lovers, we often find it hard to discover new artists and music. With snap-a-tune, you'll never be at a loss for fresh tracks that perfectly match your surroundings.

With a quick snap of your surroundings, this app utilizes photo recognition to analyze your environment and curate a playlist. snap-a-tune turns your environment into a unique musical journey, making every moment a chance to discover fresh music.

While we’re excited about snap-a-tune’s initial capabilities, we want to acknowledge that not all features are fully developed yet. Due to time constraints, we’ve had to prioritize core functionalities.

Objectives
Brainstorm

Developing an app from scratch is challenging. We gathered around a whiteboard and brainstormed various ideas, each contributing different perspectives on the problems we encounter daily and our personal interests. This collaborative effort helped us pinpoint a unique solution that combines our passion for music with practical, everyday needs.

Research Objectives
  • Investigate how users discover new music and the challenges they face in finding music that matches their current mood and environment.
  • Assess the capabilities of photo recognition and machine learning technologies to accurately analyze environments and generate relevant music playlists.
  • Examine existing music discovery apps to understand their strengths and limitations, and identify opportunities for differentiation and innovation. Determine how they help users discover new music.
  • Gather feedback on user preferences for app features and functionalities, ensuring the app is intuitive, engaging, and meets their music discovery needs.
Design Goals
  • Create a user-friendly design that facilitates easy navigation and interaction, ensuring smooth functionality between photo capture, environment analysis, and playlist generation.
  • Incorporate accessibility features to accommodate users with disabilities, such as visual and auditory impairments, ensuring an inclusive experience for all.
  • Develop a visually engaging app that enhances the user experience without overwhelming them.
  • Make discovering new music an enjoyable and effortless process, enriching the user’s connection to their surroundings.
Competitive Analysis

Using FigJam, our group conducted a comprehensive competitive analysis, leveraging sticky notes for dynamic collaboration. This approach allowed us to visually organize and assess our competitors, identify key differentiators, and strategize effectively as a team.

Next Steps

Given the limited time frame for this project, we could not finish designing all the app's features. The next steps will focus on refining the app's design, enhancing functionality, and preparing for user testing.

  • Develop and integrate the remaining app features that were not completed within the initial timeline.
  • Perform thorough usability testing. Collect feedback on both the app’s functionality and overall user experience.
  • Evaluate user feedback and address any pain points or challenges identified during testing.
  • Ensure the app meets accessibility standards by incorporating features for users with visual, auditory, or motor impairments. Conduct accessibility testing.
  • Work closely with developers to ensure that the design elements are accurately translated into the final product. Address any technical constraints if needed.
  • Develop a marketing plan and prepare for launch.
  • Post Launch: Track user interactions and identify areas for further improvement.
